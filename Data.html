Data sources and Methodoly

Our project laverages on diverse array of data sources to build and refine our AI models for Ebola detection and prediction. Below you will find detailed information on our data collection methods, types of data used, and how this data is processes and utilized in our research.

Data Sources

Healthcare Records: Anonymized patient data from healthcare facilities across affected regions , including symptom reports, treatment outcomes, and demographics. Genomic Data: Sequencing data from Ebola virus samples, providing insights into viral evolution and transmission patterns. Epidemiological Reports: Reports from public health organisations and NGOs detailing case numbers, transmision rates, and geographical spread. Environmental Data: Climate and Environmental data that may influence the spread of the virus, such as temperature, humidity and rainfall patterns.

Data Processing and Analysis

Once collected, our data undergoes rigorous processing and analysis using advance marchine learning techniques, Key steps include;-

  1. Data Cleaning: Ensuring all data is accurate, complete, and formated consistently.
  2. Data Integration: Combining data fron various sources to create comprehensive datasets.
  3. Model Training: Using the processed data to train and to validate our AI model, ensuring they are robust and accurate.
